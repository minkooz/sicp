## Solution to exercise 1.3
```lisp
(define (larger-two-square-sum x y z)
  (cond ((and (< x y) (< x z)) (+ (* y y) (* z z)))
        ((and (< y x) (< y z)) (+ (* x x) (* z z)))
        (else (+ (* y y) (* z z)))))
```
